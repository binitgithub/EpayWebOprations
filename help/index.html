E-Pay FAQ
Help and Frequently asked questions

This section contains information, related to using E-Pay and its features. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us.
Getting Started

How do I Sign Up?

Account registration is required to use E-Pay and can be done by tapping Don't have an account yet?Sign Up on the app.

I can't Sign In after Registration?

After you have registered for E-Pay, your account requires email validation, this can be done by signing in to the inbox of the email you registered with E-Pay and clicked the activation link.

I activated my account but still have trouble signing in?

If you have signed up and activated your account via email and still have trouble signing into E-Pay, please make sure you are using the correct username OR email addres and password to sign in; you can also try changing your password by tapping Forgot Password? in the app.
Mobile Wallet

How do I load my wallet?

To load your E-Pay wallet you can go to any of our authorized loading agents which can be found on the app in the Agents section E-Pay.

Can I send Funds From My Wallet?

Using E-Pay you can send and receive funds from persons using your wallet, on your wallet dashboard tap SEND to send an amount to another user OR REQUEST to ask another user for funds.
